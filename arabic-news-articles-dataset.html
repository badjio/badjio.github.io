Arabic News Articles Dataset

Fri 18 May 2018

Recently, I needed a large corpus of Arabic text for a project I was working on at the time, the text had to be clean with minimum spelling/grammatical errors. The available data sets were not enough for my use case, so I collected the data myself from multiple arabic news outlets. I published a part of it here.

It contains 140000 article with a total word count of ~39Million (avg 278 word/article).

File Structure:

A csv file with the following structure:

Title,Category,Content

Example:

إطلاق أثقل صاروخ فضائي في العالم بنجاح,sci-tech,نفذت شركة "SpaceX" الأمريكية، أول إطلاق للصاروخ الجديد...

Categories:

The articles fall into 7 categories, each with a total of 20000 article:
